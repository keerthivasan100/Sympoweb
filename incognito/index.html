INCOGNITO'24

A National Level Technical Symposium - 2024

The Symposium

Incognito 2024 is a part of Conoscenza'24 that is conducted every year, in order to bring together students from colleges across the nation in healthy competition. The sole purpose of the event is to provide a platform for students to showcase their knowledge and compete against other students. We have a range of activities and challenges that are both fun and educational for all geeks out there. It is a culmination of both, fun and technology.

The Department of IT

The Department of Information Technology offers education in undergraduate. It has a very able and skilled bunch of professors and researchers.The paramount aim is to offer the highest degree of theoretical and practical knowledge, encoded with a disciplined strength of mind to fully understand the vital sophistication of all technical and software technologies.
